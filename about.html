KNOW ABOUT US:

Growing Beyond Borders: Now Expanding Globally

Founded in October 2009 in Salem, Tamil Nadu, Eshaa Apparels began its journey under the leadership of Mrs. Lathalakshmi Dhinakaran with a clear international mission: to create world-class garments that blend quality, craftsmanship, and sustainability. In May 2010, the company evolved into a Partnership Firm, marking a milestone in its expansion. It is presently led by Mr. V. Dhinakaran (Managing Director) together with partners Mr. M. Raju, Mr. K. Mohan Kumar, Mr. R. Miller, and Mrs. R. Nagavithya. As an innovative and forward-thinking brand, Eshaa Apparels is dedicated to creating exceptional garments through technology-driven processes and ethical business practices. Our warehouse operations are powered by an advanced Warehouse Management System (WMS) to ensure precision in inventory control, order fulfillment, and lead-time optimization.

We proudly comply with global standards and are certified underscoring our commitment to ethical sourcing, organic production, and fair-trade principles. Specializing in custom-made, high-quality apparel, we cater to both domestic and international markets, delivering a versatile range of formal, semi-formal, and casual shirts as well as elegant kurthis. Our strength lies in custom design development, precise sampling, and scalable production, ensuring consistent quality and customer satisfaction in every piece we craft. Driven by a passion for excellence and responsibility, we value both our people and the planet, balancing innovation with sustainability to deliver garments that speak of comfort, confidence, and conscience.
